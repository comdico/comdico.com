Workprint

Redpony

Listen

01:Spacewalk

Main sample is from public domain audio file from the International Space Station. There are people floating in the sky above our heads.

Spacewalk

02:A Sea-Green Sea

This track combines a 90’s style wall of guitar sound with synth pop elements. Like matching patterns with stripes.

03:Empire of Glass

A drum and bass groove slowed down and played for mystery, the kind where the ending only leaves you with more questions.

04:Work Song

This track incorporates two samples from Alan Lomax field recordings from North Africa. The baseline is a re-imagining of the implied groove.

05:Hipshot

The lead guitar on the track plays a melody throughout, which was a new approach for me at the time.

06:A Prayer for Jesi

Upbeat groove searching for optimism which is somehow the kind of track that gets made when you are sad.

07:Air is Floating

This track meanders into a long coda. Floating like the state between sleep and consciousness.

08:As I Remember It

Inspired by late 80’s grooves like those from The Tom Tom Club and Kid Creole.

09:Dada in the Park

The album ends with an unapologetically upbeat tune. Also, might play well against a silent German expressionist film.

00:Album Notes

In photography a work print is a an initial print of a photograph used for evaluation and future editing. Sometimes work prints end up on museum walls as an original print of a great photographer is still a rare item.

This collection of tracks was recorded and mixed over a period of two years from many other tracks that were started and abandoned.

The completed songs here were used to understand various aspects of digital audio recording, songwriting, and musicianship.
